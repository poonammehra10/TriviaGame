Food Trivia Game

This game has a total of 5 questions to complete! Here are the details of the game:

  • Timer starts once you begin the game. You have 10 seconds to answer each question
  • Once you answer the question you will get feedback if your answer is correct or incorrect. You will be able to see this once the question as been answered.
  • If you don't answer the question before the timer runs out, it will counted it as unanswered question
  • Your total score will be displayed at the end of the game.
